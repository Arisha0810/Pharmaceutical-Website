Market Presence

International Markets

  • After being approved by the WHO-GMP certification, we are expanding our horizon to international markets.
  • The process of manufacturer registration in ASEAN countries, namely Myanmar & Cambodia, has already begun and have also initiated the process of registration.
  • Approval of our plants in African countries such as Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, Tanzania with respective Ministry of Health in the countries.
  • Discussions are underway for associations in the Latin American, the CIS and Middle Eastern countries.
