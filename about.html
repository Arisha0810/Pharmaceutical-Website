About Us

Shivalik Remedies Private Limited is a Pharmaceutical Manufacturing facility engaged in the production of formulations in General Tablets and Liquids since its inception in 2005. Right from the beginning and inception, our core value has been to provide good health by serving humanity with high quality products for affordable and accessible medical treatment.

  • Shivalik Remedies is engaged in Healthcare formulations Tablets and Liquid Orals since 2005.
  • Has been identified by several Multinational as one of their associate facilities for manufacturing quality products.

Shivalik Remedies Pvt. Ltd. is a manufacturer and exporter of pharmaceutical products.

Learn More

The Company is WHO compliant with the requisite quality certifications like GMP & GLP

Our management ensures world-class quality in everything the company offers. The plant has a proper waste management system, wherein the disposal of water is done through ETP system. We maintain more than 30% green area in our factory premises.
